About Me


Hello world! I'm Clayton Dale Tambis. a student who wants to be a web developer and loves creating beautiful and functional websites. I have experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, Python, C++, and C#, and I'm always eager to learn new technologies to enhance my skills. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new coding techniques and contributing to open-source projects.


In addition to my student life, I am an avid gamer. I have a deep passion for video games and love immersing myself in various gaming worlds. Gaming has not only been a source of entertainment for me but has also inspired me in my web development journey. The creativity, storytelling, and problem-solving aspects of games have influenced my approach to building engaging and interactive user experiences. Dancing on stages have been also one of my hobbies.


Outside of school, I love spending time in nature, exploring biking trails, and capturing beautiful moments with my camera.
